Who We Are

Built for Speed. Built for Clean.

AURA WASH was founded on a simple mission: give every driver access to a premium, fast, and affordable car wash experience. We believe your car is an extension of yourself — and it deserves to look great without the hassle of a traditional wash.

Our AI-powered wash systems, precision engineering, and eco-friendly processes deliver results that simply can't be matched by hand washing or traditional drive-throughs. Every tunnel is calibrated to your specific vehicle, every wash is optimized in real time.

We're proud to serve over 350,000 active members and millions of single-wash customers every year — and we're just getting started.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2016
The Beginning

AURA WASH opens its first location, pioneering express tunnel technology with automated vehicle scanning and precision wash calibration.

2018
Unlimited® Launch

We launch the AURA Unlimited® membership program, giving customers the ability to wash every day for one low monthly rate. Membership grows to 50,000+ in year one.

2020
100 Locations

Despite global challenges, AURA WASH reaches 100 locations across 8 states. We introduce the mobile app, contactless payment, and real-time queue status.

2022
AI Integration

Our proprietary AI wash calibration system launches across all locations. Each vehicle is scanned and measured in milliseconds, with wash parameters adjusted automatically for optimal results.

2024
Newsweek Recognition

Named to Newsweek's America's Best of the Best 2025 list. Over 300 locations, 300,000+ active members, and a 4.9-star Google average across thousands of reviews.

2026
500+ & Counting

AURA WASH surpasses 500 locations in 20+ states. The Family Plan launches, fleet accounts expand to enterprise scale, and we continue our mission to clean every car in America.
