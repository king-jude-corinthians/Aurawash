Environment

Washing Smarter, Not Harder

Traditional home car washing can use 80–140 gallons of water per wash. Our state-of-the-art reclaim systems use as little as 20 gallons per vehicle — and recycle most of that water back into the system.

In addition to water savings, AURA WASH has transitioned to solar power at over 60 of our locations, with plans to reach 100% renewable energy at all locations by 2028.
