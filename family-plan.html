How It Works

One Plan, Many Cars

The AURA Family Plan lets you link multiple vehicles to a single account. Each additional car gets a discounted membership rate, making it the smartest way to keep the whole household clean.

  • Primary vehicle pays standard membership rate
  • Each additional vehicle saves up to 40%
  • Manage all vehicles from one app account
  • Each vehicle gets its own priority lane access
  • Available on Pro Pass and Elite Pass plans
